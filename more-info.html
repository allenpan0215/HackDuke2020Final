Our Mission

FoodPrint was started to connect companies and consumers and better help them understand the environmental impact of their products. Our mission is to spread awareness of the excessive amounts of greenhouse gases that are released everyday. Each and every product we buy has gone through a process in which greenhouse gases are released when it is created, leaving a carbon footprint. These footprints contribute to the "greenhouse effect" and further global warming and climate change. We hope that our efforts encourage you to understand the products you buy and to live a more environment friendly lifestyle!
